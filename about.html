About Us

Purity, backed by a promise

Glaccia Mineral Water is produced by Infenova Enterprises — built on a simple idea: clean, safe drinking water shouldn't be a luxury.

Our Story

From Chakand to your home

Infenova Enterprises set out to solve a problem faced across Gaya and the surrounding region — reliable access to genuinely clean drinking water. Glaccia was built around that need, with a plant designed for consistency: the same purification standard in every batch, every day.

Today, Glaccia supplies households, offices and retailers with hygienically processed, multi-stage purified water — sealed, tested, and ready to trust.

Our Promise

"Ek Boond Sukoon Ki" — every drop should bring a little peace of mind. That's the standard we hold every batch to.
